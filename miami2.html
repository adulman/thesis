where you're in Miami, bitch.

WEEKEND ITINERARY

10AM JUGOFRESH

Jugofresh is a trendy juice chain with cold-pressed greens, smoothies, and bowls. A detox from last night is highly necessary.

#juiceintheair

10:30AM SOUTH BEACH

South Beach used to be a really povo area but then rich, beautiful, and gay people came in and now it's great. There are tons of Art Deco buildings, shopping, restaurants, clubs, etc. and obv the beach itself too.

11:30AM LINCOLN ROAD

Lincoln Road is a pedestrian mall with amazing shopping. You can stroll and window shop while still getting tan. #blessed

1PM JUVIA

Juvia is a restaurant on Lincoln Road with great views, food, and dranks. The food is a mix of French, Japanese, and Peruvian... idk. But it's yummy and sceney. Make sure to take a pic in the woven chairs.

2:30PM OCEAN DRIVE

The v famous main street of South Beach. Take it from Lincoln Road to the beach, beach.

Obv blast the Duke Dumont song

3:30PM NIKKI BEACH

Nikki Beach is a day and night beach club where you can darty and bullshit. By bullshit, I mean tan. Only trendy bikinis allowed.

Basic beaches

7:30PM BARTON G

Barton G is literally more of an entertainment show than a meal. Every course is theatrical - like when you order popcorn shrimp and it comes out in a fucking popcorn machine, or when your mac n cheese is inside a mousetrap. Seriously. This place is SO fun, don't miss it.

11:30PM CLUBS

Round two - gotta go even harder than last night. Check out LIV, Basement, or Story, or all three yolo.
