CHICAGO, ILLINOIS

where chi is pronounced shy, idiot

WEEKEND ITINERARY

12PM LITTLE GOAT DINER

A trendy crowd + diner vibes = next level shit. The menu is interesting and great, but obviously avoid the items with goat. Gross.

1PM SKYDECK AT WILLIS TOWER

Nothing like paying $25 and waiting in line for an hour for the perfect insta!!! No but really you should prob do this because the view is sick and you can conquer your fears by standing in a glass box a million feet over Chicago.

Waaaay up I feel... basic

2:30PM THE LOOP

The Loop is the main business district of Chi-town. It's clean and pretty. You're sure to pass a SoulCycle and a Whole Foods... home sweet home. Stop at the Chicago theatre sign for a photo op.

3:30PM GARRETT'S POPCORN

Garrett's is a popcorn chain with a "Chicago mix" that has caramel popcorn and cheddar popcorn together. Sounds disgusting, tastes amazing. Try it.

Move aside, skinny pop

4:00PM THE MAGNIFICENT MILE

All hail the Mag Mile. This is like the 5th Ave of Chicago and has everything from Topshop to Bloomies to Eataly. The more shopping bags you end up with, the better workout you'll get carrying them, so...

6:00PM JOHN HANCOCK

The John Hancock Center is a skyscraper right on the Mag Mile. Go to the restaurant and bar at the top called the Signature Room. Happy hour with a view - such a move.

Decent view

8:00PM LOU MALNATI'S

You can't go to Chicago and not eat some deep dish pizza. Lou's is the most famous. Fair warning the pizza kinda takes a while to cook (it's a literal pie...) but it's all part of the ~experience~.

Say cheese ;)

10:30PM HANGEE UPPE

God bless bars that stay open til 5AM. Downstairs is oldies sing-a-longs, but upstairs is a ratchet rager. There are multiple bars that each have special shots like watermelons or kamikazes, and plenty of elevated surfaces to dance on. Prob best to forget your night here.
