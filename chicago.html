WEEKEND ITINERARY

friday

12PM LITTLE GOAT DINER

A trendy crowd + diner vibes = next level shit. The menu is interesting and great, but obviously avoid the items with goat. Gross.

1PM SKYDECK AT WILLIS TOWER

Nothing like paying $25 and waiting in line for an hour for the perfect insta!!! No but really you should prob do this because the view is sick and you can conquer your fears by standing in a glass box a million feet over Chicago.

Waaaay up I feel... basic

2:30PM THE LOOP

The Loop is the main business district of Chi-town. It's clean and pretty. You're sure to pass a SoulCycle and a Whole Foods... home sweet home. Stop at the Chicago theatre sign for a photo op.

3:30PM GARRETT'S POPCORN

Garrett's is a popcorn chain with a "Chicago mix" that has caramel popcorn and cheddar popcorn together. Sounds disgusting, tastes amazing. Try it.

Move aside, skinny pop

4:00PM THE MAGNIFICENT MILE

All hail the Mag Mile. This is like the 5th Ave of Chicago and has everything from Topshop to Bloomies to Eataly. The more shopping bags you end up with, the better workout you'll get carrying them, so...

6:00PM JOHN HANCOCK

The John Hancock Center is a skyscraper right on the Mag Mile. Go to the restaurant and bar at the top called the Signature Room. Happy hour with a view - such a move.

Decent view

8:00PM LOU MALNATI'S

You can't go to Chicago and not eat some deep dish pizza. Lou's is the most famous. Fair warning the pizza kinda takes a while to cook (it's a literal pie...) but it's all part of the ~experience~.

Say cheese ;)

10:30PM HANGEE UPPE

God bless bars that stay open til 5AM. Downstairs is oldies sing-a-longs, but upstairs is a ratchet rager. There are multiple bars that each have special shots like watermelons or kamikazes, and plenty of elevated surfaces to dance on. Prob best to forget your night here.

saturday

10:30AM STAN'S DONUTS

Everyone knows donuts are the new cupcakes. Stan's are amazing. Get the lemon pistachio if you wanna be funky, or stick with a classic (#classic).

I donut care (about the calories) I love it

11AM CHICAGO RIVERWALK

The riverwalk is a walking path along the river. You're welcome for the explanation!!

12:30PM MILLENNIUM PARK

As overdone as the bean is, of course you need your pic there too. It's actually called Cloud Gate because of the way it reflects the sky... but the only reflection we care about is the one of ourselves, silly bean! Also check out the rest of Millenium Park and Grant Park while you're here. Then go back to the bean for round two of pics.

Just bean basic!!!!!!

2:30PM ART INSTITUTE

The Art Institute is in Millennium Park and has really famous shit. You can re-enact that scene from Ferris Bueller's Day Off (google it) and see the og goths @American Gothic.

4PM NAVY PIER

Navy Pier is a legit pier but also basically a giant playground with stores, restaurants, concert stages, theatres, a ferris wheel, a funhouse maze, mini golf, and rides. There are random festivals here on the reg and fireworks go off every Saturday night. Welcome to the Disney of Chicago.

Pro tip: instagramming a ferris wheel makes you automatically artsy

6:30PM RPM ITALIAN

The queen Giuliana Rancic is behind this restaurant and if that's not enough to make you eat there, the food is actually bomb. Order anything and everything.

8:00PM PORTILLO'S

Eating more helps food comas! Jk but one can dream. No matter how stuffed you are you must get a "cake shake" from Portillo's. It's a chocolate milkshake with an entire slice of frosted amazing chocolate cake blended in. Un fucking real.

8:00PM RIVER NORTH BARS

Once you've digested thoroughly, get drinking. The bar scene in River North is great. Hit Underground, Social Twenty-Five, and Hub 51.

Elevated surfaces AND a blurry pic, blurry night #success
