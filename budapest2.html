BUDAPEST, HUNGARY

where everything is weird but cool

WEEKEND ITINERARY

10AM STIKA

Stika is great for breakfast. The interior is trendy but still quiet and chill. Get a table by the window for optimal people watching and picture lighting.

Buda buda buda buda rockin' everywhere

11AM ST. STEPHEN'S BASILICA

St. Stephen's Basilica is a big impressive church in a really cute, central area of Budapest. You can go to the top via stairs or elevator (for the same price lol) and the view is great. V windy though - prepare your hair.

What a budaful view

12:30PM HUNGARIAN PARLIAMENT TOUR

The Hungarian Parliament puts their Opera House to shame, and that's saying something. Book the tour in advance online, and it takes about an hour. The building is incredible - so ornate and beautiful. So worth it.

1:30PM SHOES ON THE DANUBE

The Shoes on the Danube is a powerful Holocaust Memorial in between Parliament and the Chain Bridge. It just takes a walk-by to see it.

2PM CHAIN BRIDGE

Chain Bridge is the most famous bridge in Budapest, over the Danube River. It's beautiful at night and during the day, and both sides of the river bank are so pretty too.

Being basic in Budapest

2:30PM CENTRAL MARKET HALL

Central or Great Market Hall is a huge marketplace. The bottom floor has fresh fruits and vegetables and meat, but the top floor is more like food stands and flea market-y. You have to try lángos, which are fried bread frisbees that they either top with meat, cheese, and sour cream, or sweet things like nutella and fruit. We also ate beef goulash in bread bowls (@Panera honestly) and krémes, which are traditional Hungarian cakes. All amazing.

When Hungarian food is fried bread covered in sour cream and cheese, no complaints.

3:30PM GELLÉRT THERMAL BATHS

Gellért is different famous baths in Budapest, and they're a hell of a lot nicer than the Széchenyi ones. They're much smaller and more beautiful. You can get massages here too, but call in advance to book them because the schedule gets full.

5PM GELLÉRT HILL

Gellért Hill is a huge hill you can climb for a good view of the whole city. The Libery Monument is at the top.

Candid with a view

8PM TG Italiano

Tom-George Italiano has great Italian food, which is obviously why you're in Budapest right.

10:30PM SZIMPLA KERT

Szimpla Kert is the most famous ruins bar in Budapest. Ruins bars are everywhere and so cool - they're like junkyards turned into these edgy, eclectic, chill bars. Szimpla is partially inside, partially outside and so fun. Drinks are beyond cheap and they also sell random things like carrots. Just go with it.

Weird but cool - the definition of Budapest
