WEEKEND ITINERARY

friday

12PM BRUNCH & CAKE

Barcelona.... more like brunchelona. There are amazing brunch choices here so start off with one of Instagram's most popular, Brunch & Cake.

As basic as it gets

1PM CASA BATLLO

Before you read on, if there's one legit fact to know about Barc, it's that this architect named Gaudi designed everything and it all looks like it's out of Dr. Seuss or Willy Wonka. Trippy af. Casa Batllo is a sick house he designed - worth the tour.

2PM GRACIA STREET

The street that Casa Batllo is on. Casa Mila is here too, another Gaudi house. This is like the 5th Ave of Barcelona so hit up Zara because it's a Spanish company (duh) so the clothes will be better than anywhere else.

3PM LA SAGRADA FAMILIA

Another Gaudi masterpiece made even more famous by the fact that he didn't finish it. The inside has the most unreal stained glass. You can go to the park across the street to get pics with the whole church in the background.

Plot twist when the inside of a church looks like a club. Bless up

4PM PARK GUELL

OMG guess what, another Gaudi thing!!! This park has colorful mosaic benches to sit on and take pictures and other trippy shit to see. It's legit far from everything else so take a cab. Also, you need tickets. Who the fuck would think you need tickets to a park right but ya you do so buy them in advance.

Thanks @Gaudi for the photoshoot backdrop!

7PM CERVECERIA CATALANA

Trendy, crowded restaurant with long waits but worth it for the delicious and cheap tapas. You can't go out til after midnight anyways so who cares if dinner takes a while!

11PM CHUPITOS

Great pregame spot with fun shots like the Monica Lewinsky, the blowjob, the Harry Potter, shots on fire, you get the picture.

1AM CLUBS

Literally any club in Barc will be epic. Most are on the beach and you can jump between them. Do not go out before 1AM and you better stay out til 5 or 6AM or you're doing Barcelona wrong. Bonus points if you make it to see the sunrise on the beach.

saturday

10AM LAS RAMBLAS

A main drag of Barcelona. There are usually a lot of really good (and really annoying) street performers.

10:30AM LA BOQUERIA

This marketplace is right on Las Ramblas. It has tons of fresh fruits, juices, smoothies - great for breakfast. There's also pretty much anything else you want like nuts, candy, empanadas, and raw meat and fish yum!!

That good good

11:30AM GOTHIC QUARTER

This is the old, historical neighborhood of Barcelona and it's beautiful. You can walk around or do a segway tour - highly recommend this tbh it was so touristy but so great.

Even the old parts of Barc know how to party

1PM BO DE BI

Lunch place with huge lines but for a good reason. They have amazing, cheap sandwiches. Get jamon because you're in Spain.

2:30PM LA BARCELONETA

This is the beach area that you saw last night but you were drunk so that doesn't count. Check out Port Vell and just walk along the boardwalk and take in the views.

4PM CDLC

CDLC means Carpe Diem Lounge Club which is probably the greatest name ever. It's a club at night, a restaurant, but most importantly it's a great place to day drink. You can lay out on the couch beds and drink as the sun sets.

Happiness is sunglasses & sangria

7:30PM LA LUNA

A greattttt great restaurant. They have a tapas menu and a normal menu, but make sure you ask for the tapas one because the food is sick.

9:30PM LA XOCOLATERIA

The food doesn't stop. You can't leave Barc without having churros dipped in chocolate. It's mandatory.

When Europe interprets hot chocolate as literally melted chocolate >>>

1AM OPIUM

Probably the most famous of all the beach clubs. Opium is unreal - crazy music and lights, and it's pretty big. Don't lose your friends or your phone but definitely lose your mind.




















sunday

10AM PICASSO MUSEUM

Fittingly this place has the biggest collection of Picasso so it's pretty cool to see it all together. Spend as much or as little time as you'd like.

11:30AM ARC DE TRIOMF

Like Paris but not. Guys in front of this sell the fun rainbow bubble wands, so, photo op!

12:30PM HAPPY PILLS

The famous candy store. If you haven't heard of this, how are you possibly reading this website? Grab some candy for your plane/train/whatever out of Barc and obviously keep the empty jar after.

*Jappy pills
