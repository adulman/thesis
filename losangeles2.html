WEEKEND ITINERARY

10AM URTH CAFFE

Legit the opposite of being down to urth/earth but whatevs. Celebs love this place, and they ate here on Entourage like every five episodes. The menu is huge with breakfast bowls, eggs, salads, sandiwches, pizza - anything you want, as long as you also get boba or some green tea drink too.

The only urth that betches care about

10:30AM SANTA MONICA PIER

A classic LA landmark. There's an amusement park with a ferris wheel, and other random shit like an aquarium. Walk along the pier or on the beach to check it out.

11:30AM THIRD STREET PROMENADE

The promenade is a little touristy but yolo you're only in LA for the weekend. It's a pedestrian mall and v pretty with great shopping and people watching.

LA LA land

1PM BAY CITIES

Bay Cities is a newly basic lunch place in Santa Monica. It has great Italian sandwiches. Native LA peeps may not eat gluten but you're a visitor and you still do, so enjoy it for them.

4PM VENICE BEACH

Let's go to the beach, beach. Venice is v nice. There are also artsy graffiti walls to take pics in front of, and sk8r bois to judge from behind your sunglasses.

Beach, please

7:30PM SUGARFISH

More suuuuush. Tbh LA's sushi is so much better than anywhere else that you should literally be eating it up as much as possible. Sugarfish is an institution and much more authentic than other places. Real fuckin deal.

9:30PM BEACHY BARS

SHOREbar is a beachy bar lounge with delicious high end cocktails and a DJ. It's cute and fun. The Bungalow is a boho retreat with a hipster-feeling inside and a beautiful outside deck. They have margs and "bitchin' sangria," obviously the perfect thing to order when you're with your bitches.

Get the "bitchin' sangria" at Bungalow, sit back, and bitch with your bitches.
