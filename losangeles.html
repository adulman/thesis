LOS ANGELES, CALIFORNIA

where kale is its own food group and celebs are just like us

WEEKEND ITINERARY

12PM JOAN'S ON THIRD

Joan's is the shit. It's a gourmet market and restaurant with salads, soups, sandwiches, and beautiful people. The food is bomb and the environment is even better - you don't even have to feel any shame about how basic you're being.

TG for the opportunity to insta your sandals

1PM LACMA

If you're gonna do something cultural in LA, better to choose a trendy art museum than gross tar pits. The Los Angeles County Museum of Art is more known for the columns / lamp posts outside it than the art in it, but classic LA where outside appearances count more than inside.

HAHA, look how fun these columns are!!

2PM RODEO DRIVE

The fuckin mecca. You can shop on Robertson or Melrose too, but Rodeo is the best. Don't miss the iconic Beverly Hills Hotel at one end - you'll recognize it from movies and shit. Beverly Hills, that's where I wanna be...

3:30PM DIDDY RIESE

An amazing shop with fresh, warm cookies. They seriously must put crack in the cookies to make them so incredible. You can mix and match what cookies and what ice cream you want to make a custom ice cream sandwich. Lines can be annoyingly long, but they move fast and the cookies are v cheap so it's highly worth it.

Next level shit

4:30PM MALIBU COUNTRY MART

How quaint, naming a high end shopping plaza a “country mart”. WTF? Hit up Ron Herman, Kitson, and the Coffee Bean to get your fill of LA brands.

7:30PM NOBU

Go to Nobu to eat the best sushi on a beautiful patio looking out over the Pacific. It doesn't get better (or scenier) than this.

Giving new meaning to the California roll

11PM BARS ON SUNSET

Sunset Strip, ever heard of it? Head to the bars and clubs here to spot celebs. Blind Dragon is an Asian themed lounge where you can even do karaoke if you're drunk enough. Bootsy Bellows is next door and more ratchet, with burlesque dancers and pinup vibes. Snapchat the glowing pink and red sign or you were never there.
