PARIS, FRANCE

where the shopping is great and the food is even better.

WEEKEND ITINERARY

9:30AM CAFE ANGELINA

Early wake up call, sorry, but the hot chocolate and pastries at Cafe Angelina will be worth it. It’s a little pricey but you’ll feel like Eloise in the fucking Plaza, pardon my French.

10:30AM THE LOUVRE

Visiting Paris without seeing the Mona Lisa is rude. Yes, she’s a little unimpressive in person, but you can still tell your Mom you saw her. Also, the Louvre is huge and full of other famous shit, so you can walk off your breakfast while getting cultured. Win win.

Visiting paris without seeing the Mona Lisa is rude

12PM CHAMPS-ELYSEES

When you exit the Louvre, walk through the Tuileries gardens and they’ll lead you to the 5th Avenue of France: The Champs-Elysees. Shop til you drop (every dollar to your name) at Hermes, Louis Vuitton, Longchamp, Chanel, Goyard, Van Cleef & Arpels, and every other top French brand. Careful not to step on the lines of Asians. If you’re hungry, grab some macarons from Laduree or a baguette sandwich from Paul.

2PM ARC DE TRIOMPHE

At the end of the Champs, that giant arch is the Arc de Triomphe. I think it’s pretty important and historical in some way. More importantly it’s also the best view in Paris. Climb it and you’ll see all of Paris, including the Eiffel Tower. Hopefully the 200+ stairs will help you say au revoir to all the baguettes and crepes you’ve been eating.

4PM MONTMARTRE & SACRE COEUR

Metro to the Montmartre area of Paris. You can take the cable car up the hill from the Abesses metro stop, or climb the stairs (beware - it’s a lot). At the top, there’s a square full of artists painting portraits and selling their work. It’s really, really cute. Sacre Coeur, another famous church, is here too. Grab a bottle of wine and watch the sun set over Paris from the hill in front of it.

7PM LE REFUGE DES FONDUS

You’re already drunk so might as well keep it going. This hole-in-the-wall restaurant in Montmartre is an abroadee favorite. Stuff your face with fondue and drink wine out of a baby bottle, because wine not? Don’t leave without signing your name on the wall.

Wine not drink out of a baby bottle?

10PM SHOWCASE & SOCIAL CLUB

No judgement if you feel the need to pass out at this point, but if you’re up for it, head to Showcase for a true European clubbing experience. End your night at Social Club. Both are crowded, sweaty, and hold the promise of amazing stories, if you don’t lose your phone.
