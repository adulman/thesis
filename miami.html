WEEKEND ITINERARY

12PM MS. CHEEZIOUS

A grilled cheese heaven. The menu includes mac n cheese on sourdough, a buffalo chicken melt, a pesto and tomato melt, and a s'mores melt, but if none of those work for you, you can also build your own. Gasp.

Gimme s'more of that

1PM WYNWOOD WALLS

It doesn't get more basic than the Wynwood Walls. It's an area of old warehouses converted to graffiti and street art. There are tons of insta backdrops to choose from, but the most classic is this one.

Peace, love, Wynwood Walls

2PM WOOD TAVERN

Wood Tavern is a super chill bar with a patio area full of picnic tables and cool lights and art. Grab a beer and soak in the vibes.

3PM THE SALTY DONUT

A trendy donut place. They have donut holes that come with syringes to inject the donut with different flavors. Amazing.

4PM PAMM

Perez Art Museum Miami is a beautiful building right by the water and has a solid collection of modern art. There are cool hanging gardens on the patio, and you can say you did something more cultural than just beach and shopping on your trip.

Wynwood Walls 2.0!?

7PM JOE'S STONE CRAB

A classic Miami restaurant with amazing seafood and steak. It's a little pricey, but welcome to Miami. Great service, atmosphere, and food. Don't skip the key lime pie.

11PM E11EVEN

A sick club with all the usuals - house music, crazy lights. It's kinda ratchet like a Cirque du Soleil strip club but when in Miami...

You can find me in the club, bottle full of bub
