where you're in Miami, bitch.

WEEKEND ITINERARY

10AM THREEFOLD CAFE

Threefold is home of the basics. The vibes are cozy and bright, and the lighting for instas is on point. The avo toast comes on bread from Zak the Baker which is a bfd in Miami.

No surprises here

10:30AM MIRACLE MILE

Why is there a miracle mile in like every city? This one is in downtown Coral Gables, home to UMiami. Shop away!

11:30AM COCONUT GROVE

Right next to Coral Gables, and equally beloved by UMiami kids. The Grove has a bunch of restaurants and bars by the water. Walk around the Village Center or docks.

1PM MONTY'S RAW BAR

Monty's is right on the water in Coconut Grove and the most stunning scene. The food is solid but you're really here for the view and the yachts.
