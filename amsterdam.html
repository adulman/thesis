AMSTERDAM, THE NETHERLANDS

where it's a carnival for high people

WEEKEND ITINERARY

11AM BAKERS & ROASTERS

Welcome to Amsterdam, let's get eating. Bakers & Roasters has great brunch and great lighting. Get the latkes benedict (yeah) or the banana bread french toast (yeah again).

AmsterDAMN

12PM BULLDOG

Starting Amsterdam at the Hard Rock of coffee shops. FYI a "coffee shop" means a place that sells weed, and you'll need your ID to buy some. Bulldog is touristy af, so also check out Easy Times and Dolphins, but only after you've bought a Bulldog souvenir.

Feelin trippy already

1PM LEIDSEPLEIN

The area Bulldog is in is a great little square with stores and restaurants. Watch out for the bikers and trams that go through, though - shit's dangerous when you're high.

1:30PM JAMIN & NUTELLA ICE BAKERY

Amsterdam is like a carnival for high people. Go to Jamin - a candy store that also sells waffles, ice cream, etc, and make sure to buy some stroopwafels - amazing caramel cookie things all over Amsterdam. Nutella Ice Bakery has waffles, crepes, muffins, anything you want, topped with nutella. Unreal.

A picture is worth 1,000 calories

2PM CANAL TOUR

Amsterdam is made up of tons of canals and they're all gorgeous. Hop on a boat tour and be transported around them, hearing about the city's history and shit. Zero effort and so many photo ops.

3:30PM BLOEMENMARKT

Bloemenmarkt means "flower market" in Dutch. V explanatory! Walk around all the flower vendors - it's incredible. If you still want to be high, stop by Mellow Yellow on the way there for great edibles.

Flower power

4:30PM CHIPSY KING

The best french fry stand in Amsterdam. Fries b4 guys!! Order with mayo if you wanna be authentic.

5:30PM HEINEKEN FACTORY

Pregame dinner with a few beers from the Heineken Factory. The tour is really cool and interactive, and comes with 3 free beers so...

Amsterdam: where even their beer bottles are green

8PM MOMO

Great sushi and cocktails at the Park Hotel. The vibes are super stylish but still chill. Both tourists and locals love it here.
