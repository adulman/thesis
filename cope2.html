COPENHAGEN, DENMARK

where the happiest (and coldest) people are

WEEKEND ITINERARY

9:30AM THE UNION KITCHEN

Copenhagen’s brunch game is strong. This place has avocado toast and cappuccinos with foam art - it will make your basic heart feel right at home.

Being basic is a full time job

11AM TRAMPOLINES

Burn off your brunch on this street that has trampolines built into the sidewalk. It will make you understand why Cope has the rep for being the happiest city.

11:30AM NYHAVN HARBOR

The most famous sight in Copenhagen. This is the harbor with rainbow houses. Enough said.

@America why aren’t you this pretty?

12:30PM AMALIENBORG PALACE

The Royal Danish family lives here. You can watch the changing of the guard and admire the architecture. Culture!

2PM LAGKAGEHUSET

The other must-try bakery in Copenhagen. Get the chocolate pastry here - it’s insane. Maybe buy two because you’ll want a second.

3PM HAPPY WALL

If you live on Mars and haven’t heard of this, it’s a public art installation where you can flip tiles on a giant wall to spell out anything your heart desires. Examples: hi mom, go blue, sorority letters, you get the picture (literally).

4PM CHRISTIANIA

This is the hippie village of Copenhagen. Weed is legal, so everyone goes here to buy and smoke. There are picnic tables, little shops, a lake, and lots of cool graffiti. Stay for the sunset, but peace before it’s really dark because it can get creepy.

Nothing says #trendy like a pic in front of a graffiti wall

7PM NEIGHBOURHOOD

Great pizzas and salads, and not too expensive, which is hard to find in Cope. It's all good in the neighbourhood.

11PM BAR7

A trendy, crowded bar with fun cocktails. There are a bunch of rooms and a DJ playing on the ground floor. Both Americans and Danes love it here.
