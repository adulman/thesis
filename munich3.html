WEEKEND ITINERARY

10AM BMW MUSEUM

The BMW Welt is the show room. You can walk around it for free and take pictures or get in the cars. The BMW museum itself is 7 euros, but really well done and cool.

11:30AM OLYMPIAPARK

Olympiapark is a huge park that was built for the Olympic games one year (who cares when). It's nice to walk around, and you can take the elevator to the top of the Olympiaturm (the tall tower) for a decent view of the whole park and whole city.
