AMSTERDAM, THE NETHERLANDS

where it's a carnival for high people

WEEKEND ITINERARY

9AM ANNE FRANK HOUSE

You have to do at least one real thing that isn't smoking or eating in Amsterdam. Buy tickets in advance for this, or get in line at 8AM. Good to do it first thing in the morning so you're not high yet out of respect.

10:30AM COFFEE SHOPS

Hit up Dampkring or Resin. Resin has swings inside which are so fun.

11AM THE PANCAKE BAKERY

The Pancake Bakery is next to the Anne Frank House and a can't miss. The lines can be long but the pancakes are amazing - get both sweet and savory. The giant omelettes rock too.

(Pan)cake cake cake

12:30PM TULIP MUSEUM

A small place on the same street as Pancake Bakery where you can learn about why Amsterdam is so obsessed with tulips. And insta the pretty flowers too.

1PM CHEESE MUSEUM

Next door to the Tulip Museum, the Cheese Museum is more of a cheese shop than a museum but still hilarious. It has unlimited free samples (!!!!!) and hilarious props to take pictures with. Start brainstorming your cheesy captions now.

Sometimes you just rlly need to hug a few wheels of cheese

1:30PM WINKEL 43

A restaurant known for its amazing Dutch apple pie. Worth stopping in for a quick slice with ice cream or whipped cream.

Say crack again

2:30PM DAM SQUARE

The main square in Amsterdam. There's a giant Dutch clog you can climb into to take pictures. They also hold different festivals here so you might run into a fun event while you're in Amsterdam.

4PM RED LIGHT DISTRICT

Really funny and weird to walk around. Naked women basically stand in windows and dance or beckon for you to come in, but you're just like, what? It's also legit lit up red which is cool. You can see a sex show or visit the SexMuseum if you want. Don't go at night, and don't take any pictures - it's illegal apparently.

Red Light District is its own filter

7PM BARNEY'S UPTOWN

Barney's is a coffeeshop with a few locations. Uptown is a cool bar / lounge / smokeshop scene with a DJ. They're also known for their burgers. Hang out and get high for the comedy show next.

10:30PM BOOM CHICAGO

BOOM Chicago is an American comedy show troupe in Amsterdam, prob there just to cater to high American tourists. It's decently funny and a good nighttime high activity. Buy tickets in advance if you can!
