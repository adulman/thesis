WEEKEND ITINERARY

9AM ANNE FRANK HOUSE

BUY TIX IN ADVANCE they sell out like months in advance so even if you’re thinking like oh I mightttt go to Amsterdam this weekend - just buy the tix We didn’t have tix and we stood in line for 1.5 hours (it opens at 9, we got in line at 8 and got in by 9:30) Worth it though the Anne Frank house is cool to see

11AM THE PANCAKE BAKERY

Next to Anne Frank House There can be long lines but luckily there was no wait when we went The pancakes are great (both sweet and savory) and the giant omelettes were really good too

12:30PM TULIP MUSEUM

Right next door to the Cheese Museum but we didn’t do it

1PM CHEESE MUSEUM

Right down the street from Anne Frank Takes 10 min to do the actual museum, get some funny pics The upstairs is more of a cheese store but it’s unlimited free samples!!!!

1:30PM WINKEL 43

Down the street from Cheese Museum Known for amazing Dutch apple pie

2:30PM DAM SQUARE

The main square in Amsterdam There’s a giant Dutch clog you can climb into and take pics The weekend I was there, there was a festival with rides and games and food and stuff in this square - so fun

4PM RED LIGHT DISTRICT

Funny and weird to walk around Sex shows are expensive so we didn’t do it, we just walked around the streets instead and saw it Don’t take pics!!! It’s illegal. Also don’t go at night!!!!!! Unless you’re with a big group / boys and feeling brave There’s a sexmuseum here but we didn’t do it

hi

7PM BARNEY'S UPTOWN

There’s 3 locations - we went to Barney’s Uptown one night and it was a cool bar/lounge/smokeshop scene…. DJ played music, we got drinks and smoked and hung out there all night

10:30PM BOOM CHICAGO

American comedy show, pretty fun - not the most hilarious thing ever but it’s a good night time high activity Buy tix in advance if you can
