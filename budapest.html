BUDAPEST, HUNGARY

where everything is weird but cool

WEEKEND ITINERARY

12PM HUMMUS BAR

Hummus Bar will be your favorite hello and your hardest goodbye of Budapest. It's a cheap chain restaurant with great Mediterranean food like falafel, hummus, and shwarma. Hummus is yummus.

Yummus Bar

1PM ANDRÁSSY STREET

Andrássy is the main avenue of Budapest. It has good shopping and a lot of the big sights, like the Opera House, the House of Terror, Heroes' Square, yada yada.

1:30PM HUNGARIAN OPERA HOUSE

The Hungarian Opera House is BEAUTIFUL. You can do a walk-by and see it from the outside, or take a quick tour inside - do it. The place is dripping in gold and velvet. Drooool.

Rollin' out the red carpet because you're in Budapest

2PM HOUSE OF TERROR

The House of Terror is a scary fuckin name but it's actually a really interesting museum about the history of communism and fascism in Hungary. Ya who knew they had a crazy past of prisoners and torture and stuff. Not me, but it was interesting to learn about. It's kinda heavy but you can choose how quickly you want to walk through it (aka, get outta there).

3PM HEROES' SQUARE

Heroes' Square is at the bottom of Andrássy Street and v iconic. It has a bunch of sculptures and monuments, the usual. Right behind the square is the entrance to City Park, where you can walk around the castles, go to the zoo, go ice skating if it's winter, and most importantly, hit up the baths.

We could be heroes...

4PM SZÉCHENYI THERMAL BATHS

OBVIOUSLY the number one destination in Budapest is the Széchenyi baths. Budapest has a bunch of public thermals baths but these are the biggest and most famous, with lots of indoor and outdoor pools. You have to go, but tbh we were very skeeved out by the whole experience. It's kinda dirty and confusing, shoutout to the Hungarians for not having any signs in English - so rude. It's just a total maze and you might leave with a disease. But none of this should deter you from going and getting an Instagram, of course!!

Smiling despite all the randos and germs!!!

7:30PM KÖNYVBÁR & RESTAURANT

This is one of my favorite restaurants in all of Europe. “Könyv” means “book” in Hungarian, and the restaurant looks like a cozy library inside. Every week, the menu changes based on a different book. So fucking cool. The owner is half American and the friendliest guy, and the food is actually amazing. Honestly so jealous you're in Budapest and going here...

The BudaBEST restaurant ever

9:30PM INSTANT

Instant is a cool mix between a bar and a club. There's no cover (#blessed) and tons of different rooms for whatever vibes you're feeling.

11:30PM HELLO BABY

Hello Baby is a crazy nightclub on Andrássy. It's in like a palace - classic Europe. There are two main dance floors with different music, and the whole place is combo classy and out of control. Perfection.
