BUDAPEST, HUNGARY

where everything is weird but cool

WEEKEND ITINERARY

12PM HUMMUS BAR

Chain restaurant we went to for lunch - there’s multiple locations Sooooo cheap - get falafel, hummus, shwarma etc

Hummus bar

1PM ANDRASSY STREET

This is the main avenue in Budapest with good shopping, and a lot of sights - Opera House, Heroes Square, etc

1:30PM HUNGARIAN OPERA HOUSE

Beautiful building on Andrassy Street We just did a walk by and saw it from the outside, but you can take a tour inside if you want You can see an opera if you want - people said it’s cheap and fun

Opera house

2PM HOUSE OF TERROR

Interesting museum on Andrassy Street about the history of communism and fascism in Hungary, and prisoners/torture stuff A little heavy, but did teach you a lot about Hungarian history Only takes approx an hour to get through - it’s mainly in Hungarian not English Get the audio guide if they have them available

3PM HEROES SQUARE

Square at the bottom of Andrassy Street with lots of sculptures/monument things - very iconic

heroes square

4PM SZECHENYI THERMAL BATHS

city park.Huge park behind Heroes Square - walk around all the castles Huge ice skating park here if you go in the winter The zoo is here too Szechenyi Baths are here Public thermal baths - the most famous ones / biggest - there’s so many indoor and outdoor pools Everyone goes here when they visit Budapest but honestly we were pretty skeeved out by it… it’s just kinda dirty and smelly and public…. and confusing - all the signs are in Hungarian and the place is a gigantic maze If you go, bring a towel (you can rent one for a fee but may as well bring one from your hotel) and a bathing suit and flip flops You can get massages here - not too expensive The entrance fee to the baths is $15-20 I believe

szechenyi

7:30PM KONYVBAR & RESTAURANT

AMAZING restaurant “Konyv” is the hungarian word for “book” - books decorate the restaurant everywhere, and each week in addition to their set menu they make a menu based on a different book. It’s so cool The owner is half American and the sweetest guy And the food is UNREAL get the goat cheese app, the corn soup, the tomato basil soup, lamb chops, pastas, everything Also great drinks

konyvbar

9:30PM INSTANT

Instant - mix between club and bar

11:30PM HELLO BABY

Hello Baby - crazy nightclub near the Oktogon
