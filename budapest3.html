where everything is weird but cool

WEEKEND ITINERARY

10AM JEWISH QUARTER

dohany street synagogue 2nd largest synagogue in the world and so beautiful Show up at the synagogue and go on one of the tours they offer like every 30 min Different tour options - just the synagogue, the synagogue and the museum, or both of those and a walking tour of the Jewish Quarter - just pick which one you want! The Jewish Quarter has amazing food options too

dohany

11:30AM CASTLE DISTRICT

buda castle, castle hill, matthias church, fisherman's bastion

Buda Palace is here, Matthias Church, Fisherman’s Bastion - all on the Buda side of the city Very cute area to walk around Matthias Church is beautiful Fisherman’s bastion is a lookout point on the whole city (we did this instead of Gellert) To get up here - cross the Chain Bridge, and then you can walk up the windy ramp to Buda Palace or take the funicular gondola thing for pretty cheap
