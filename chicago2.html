CHICAGO, ILLINOIS

where chi is pronounced shy, idiot

WEEKEND ITINERARY

10:30AM STAN'S DONUTS

Everyone knows that donuts are the new cupcakes. Stan's are amazing. Get the lemon pistachio if you wanna be funky, or stick with a classic (#classic).

I donut care (about the calories) I love it

11AM CHICAGO RIVERWALK

The riverwalk is a walking path along the river. You're welcome for the explanation!!

12:30PM MILLENNIUM PARK

As overdone as the bean is, of course you need your pic there too. The bean is actually called Cloud Gate because of the way it reflects the sky... but the only reflection we care about is the one of ourselves, silly bean! Also check out the rest of Millennium Park and Grant Park while you're here. Then go back to the bean for round two of pics.

Just bean basic!!!!!!

2:30PM ART INSTITUTE

The Art Institute is in Millennium Park and has really famous shit. You can re-enact that scene from Ferris Bueller's Day Off (google it) and see the og goths @American Gothic.

4PM NAVY PIER

Navy Pier is a legit pier but also basically a giant playground with stores, restaurants, concert stages, theatres, a ferris wheel, a funhouse maze, mini golf, and rides. There are random festivals here on the reg and fireworks go off every Saturday night. Welcome to the Disney of Chicago.

Pro tip: instagramming a ferris wheel makes you automatically artsy

6:30PM RPM ITALIAN

The queen Giuliana Rancic is behind this restaurant and if that's not enough to make you eat here, the food is actually bomb. Order anything and everything.

8:30PM PORTILLO'S

Plot twist, eating more actually helps food comas! Jk but one can dream. No matter how stuffed you are you must get a “cake shake” from Portillo's. It's a chocolate milkshake with an entire slice of frosted amazing chocolate cake blended in. Un fucking real.

10PM RIVER NORTH BARS

Once you've digested thoroughly, get drinking. The bar scene in River North is great. Hit Underground, Social Twenty-Five, and Hub 51.

Elevated surfaces AND a blurry pic, blurry night #success
