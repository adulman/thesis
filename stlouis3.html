WEEKEND ITINERARY

10AM TAVERN

Great restaurant. Save room for dessert, because they're most known for their warm chocolate chip cookie skillet topped with cookie dough ice cream and peanut butter mousse. Yep, you read that right.

I feel bad for every dessert that isn't this.

11:30AM CENTRAL WEST END

Tavern is located in the Central West End, a trendy neighborhood with a bunch of stores and restaurants. Walk down Euclid Ave between Washington Blvd and Forest Park Ave before you peace STL.
