MUNICH, GERMANY

where beer is the new water

WEEKEND ITINERARY

12PM VIKTUALIENMARKT

This is a really cool open air market in the city center. There are souvenir shops, food stands, little flower markets, and a beer garden in the middle of the whole thing because Germany.

Am I artsy yet?

2:30PM MARIENPLATZ

Marienplatz is the city center. There are a bunch of cute streets with shopping, churches, markets, etc. The buildings are v pretty, especially the new city hall.

4:30PM ST. PETER'S CHURCH

Right in the city center, and 300 steps to the top for a view of the whole city. It's the cliiiiimb....

Munich from the top

6PM WEISSES BRAUHAUS

A more traditional German restaurant right near St. Peter's Church. Unclear what things actually are but it all tastes good. Def get strudel for dessert.

Munching in Munchen

4PM HOFBRAUHAUS

Hofbrauhaus is a famous, huge beer hall in the city center with live German music and endless beer. Pregame your night here and absorb the German culture.

4PM KULFTABRIK

Kulftabrik is an area of warehouses that were converted into like 40 different bars and clubs. There is a lot of cool graffiti and artsy vibes here. Jump around all of the places but you'll prob feel most at home at Americanos. Yes.

Soft smiles to try to fit in with the Germans
