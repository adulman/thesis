ST. LOUIS, MISSOURI

where the people are nice and the food is better

WEEKEND ITINERARY

10AM SOULARD FARMER'S MARKET

In the Soulard neighborhood, there's a huge farmers market with great produce. It's fun to walk around in general, try the samples, and people watch the fat St. Louisians.

11:30AM PAPPY'S SMOKEHOUSE

Head to Pappy's to get on line for the lunch rush. STL is known for its great BBQ and Pappy's is prob the most famous of them all. Buy a t-shirt while you're there - “Yeah dude, we smoke” is a great souvenir for your stoner friend.

Nice to meat you, St. Louis BBQ

2PM THE ARCH

The gateway to the west!!!!!!! The Arch is kinda cool to see and by see I mean get a picture of. You can buy tickets to go up to the top, but only if you're okay with a janky and claustrophobic elevator ride. If not, the best pic is taken from the Old Courthouse.

When the Arch is too tall to fit in the pic #adversity

6PM BAILEYS RANGE

Baileys is a trendy restaurant downtown with burgers and shakes. The burgers are all super interesting (I mean that in a good way). They also have like 10 different sauce options for the fries so that's fun.

8:30PM THREE SIXTY

Three Sixty is a rooftop bar with, you guessed it, 360 degree views of STL. It's a good scene and the drinks are solid.

10:30PM BALLPARK VILLAGE

Ballpark Village, or BPV, is an area of bars and restaurants next to Busch Stadium. The bars are so fun - go to Howl at the Moon for the live music and drink specials, or PBR to feel the ~midwestern~ vibes and ride the mechanical bull.

Betches love bucket drinks

2:30AM JOHN DONUT

Finish your night with a donut (or two) from John's. The owner is an asshole and the place is bizarre but the donuts are dirt cheap and amazing. Get the apple fritter (a must) and then anything else of your choice.
